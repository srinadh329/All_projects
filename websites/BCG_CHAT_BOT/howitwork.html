Virtual Assistant Technology

Creating a virtual assistant involves three key elements that effectively help users and parallelly delivers measurable results to their business.

Our Artificial Intelligence has one of the best Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Natural Language Understanding (NLU) processes in the world. Additionally, our system has automated learning that helps us improve our programs constantly.

How Does It Work?

  • User input
  • Pre-processing
  • Intent prediction
  • Dynamic response
  • Anonymisation and privacy
  • Analysis and optimisation

User input

User inputs are considered as communication features and are proficient for messaging. Based on the user inputs, intents and entities will be classified on NLU.

Pre-processing

Messages received from the users will be sent to Natural Language Understanding for pre-processing the content. Pre-processing at NLU goes through several techniques that involve removal of irrelevant keywords and create clear statements.

NLU pre-processes the data by splitting the words, detecting the language and correcting the spellings.

Intent prediction

Based on the user's input, our virtual assistant understands user's intention and fetches relevant information through training data. Multiple intent support makes the user key- in different statements at a time, which gives the user a feeling of having a meaningful conversation with a human than a bot.

The performance of our model depends on training and user behaviour. After training the model, it is subjected to analyse the user's behaviour in order to provide accurate answers.

Dynamic response

Post prediction of the accurate intent, our assistant resolutely provides a dynamic response.

There are two kinds of responses:

One is derived from the text that is already pre-defined and the other is by providing complex answers. The complex answers and actions can perform advanced tasks using entity extraction and external APIs.

Adding support to advanced responses makes the virtual assistant interact with authenticated users.

Anonymisation and privacy

The last step of the messaging platform is the removal of personal data. We provide an adequate level of security and confidentiality to your personal data, taking into account the nature and risk of processing such data.

We assure that your data is processed with privacy and security, including anonymisation.

Consecutively, we develop and implement better ways to preserve the privacy of our users.

Analysis and optimisation

Subsequent to the conclusion of the user's dialogue, our Bot analyses the data using analytic tool.

These tools perform intensive analysis of all the conversations between users and bots. It measures the effectiveness and expectations of each message, whilst suggesting optimisation in a convenient and clean graphical interface.

Furthermore, these tools will help you gain wider insights and analysis about your customers and their experience with the Bot.
