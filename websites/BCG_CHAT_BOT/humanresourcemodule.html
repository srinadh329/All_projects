Virtual Human Resources

Our virtual agent for HR is able to streamline and personalize the HR process across seasonal, temporary, part-time and full-time employees. It allows applicants to ask questions and get quick automated answers while perusing the company's website.

Conversational AI in human resources has truly given professionals the means to utilize their time on the more strategic and pressing elements while spending less time on operational issues.

The bot uses natural language processing, artificial intelligence and optimized search and has over 4,000 responses to different questions. It uses AI to check a candidate’s qualifications to make sure they’re a good match with a company before connecting them.

The data gained through AI can help HR teams recruit new employees as well as retaining and motivating existing employees. It uses old and historical data to provide solutions and resolve issues thereby assisting HR leaders in creating focused individual programs and allow applicants to ask questions and get quick automated answers.

4000 user intents

Our agent includes 4000 plus intents that cover topics and queries related to recruitment, on-boarding, FAQs on company policies, leave, salary etc.

Sentiment analysis

With natural language processing and continuous training, our agent can also evaluate, measure and understand employee sentiment accurately in order to assist organizations in finding and hiring applicants.

Prediction models

Helps in making the application process more transparent. If applicants have questions about job descriptions, company details or potential responsibilities they can simply ask the bot and are good to go.

Built on experience

Our agent is truly a self-serve process as they have the ability to talk to employees onsite and interact with workforce management.
