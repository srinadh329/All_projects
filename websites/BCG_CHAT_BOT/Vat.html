Virtual assistant training

Only a few training messages required
We can train with a few user utterances for prediction of the intent in short time. A large number of datasets are not required for training the bot for performing the task.
Training data templates available
The default templates in the platform which are trained help the end users to import the template into the platform by selecting specific domain that facilitates the one-click creation of the bot of their requirement. Further, we can enhance the bot by adding our custom training data to the default template which improves the efficiency.
Custom industry vocabulary
The custom industry vocabulary helps to improve the performance and increase the training rate of the bot by adding a knowledge base to the bot for better Natural Language Understanding of that selected domain (Ex: Telecom, Railway).
