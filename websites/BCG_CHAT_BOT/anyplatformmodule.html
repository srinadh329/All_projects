Any Platform

Messaging apps will soon leverage on technologies such as chatbot to deliver smarter and better engagement with the customers and being able to reach a broad audience on messenger apps, as well as the ability to automate personalized messages.

Users have their core apps such as Facebook, Whatsapp, Messenger etc. Therefore, integrating the chatbot into one of the popular platforms can be better than building a new app and let businesses have the opportunity to reach more customers.

Our bot allows interaction through texting, voice and even facebook messenger allowing users an easy to use ordering experience through their mobile phones.

Messaging apps as bots offer everything from customer service to online shopping and banking.

Rapid growth in messenger apps

Users are spending more time on messaging apps like Messenger, Whatsapp, Skype, WeChat and others. Using chatbots in messaging platforms allow brands to interact personally.

Interaction with channels

We configured our bot to connect to the channels you want it to be available such as Cortana, Facebook Messenger, Kik and Slack, as well as several others.

Reaching new customers

Being continuously available on platforms like Facebook Messenger helps companies reach new customers.

Adaptable framework

Allows you to develop your bot in a channel-agnostic way by normalizing messages that the bot sends to a channel. This involves converting it from the bot builder schema into the channel’s schema.
