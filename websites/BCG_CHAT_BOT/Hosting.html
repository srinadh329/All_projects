Hosting

Cloud

Hosting on the cloud enables the services of the bot all over the world.

So that it can be accessible from anywhere in the world and provides services to the end users with more scalability and elasticity.

On-premise
Chatbots deal with a lot of confidential business data. While hosting them on a third-party cloud can bring efficiency, hosting them on-premise gives sufficient room for customization and helps you monitor your data access and usage more closely. These chatbots reduce your dependency on vendors by offering greater flexibility and protect your long-term interests. Besides, they cut down the ongoing costs of hosting.
