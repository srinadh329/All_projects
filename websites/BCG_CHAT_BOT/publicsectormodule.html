Virtual Public Sector

Virtual assistants in the public sector are gaining its momentum. It allows an authority to quickly solve citizens as well as city problems and acts as a direct communication medium between government to citizens.

Our virtual assistant with it rich interface and premade intents gives citizens an accessible, up-to-date calendar of events. The existence of chatbots brings all of this information into one chat window that they can open up at any time.

Actions such as renewal of driving licenses, applications for pensions or other relief packages and filing and payment of taxes can be initiated online via live chat, thus taking less time and requiring less manpower on the part of the government bodies involved.

Provides multilingual support. People can get assistance in their native language and also can get support on multiple platforms.

Quick access to public data

The virtual assistant can serve as a reliable contact point for information that your citizens seek on a regular basis. Notifications about upcoming local events, activities and holidays make up a significant percentage of this information.

Built in with 4000 user intents

Our 4000 unique intents are designed to send messages and reminders to new hires with information about forms, discussions and the ever-present government jargon.

Reducing employees workload

Being able to provide simple responses to direct questions renewal of driving licenses, applications for pensions, filing and payment of taxes can be initiated online via live chat.

Providing 24/7 City Guide

It provides 24/7 availability and high accessibility to citizens. It can interact with citizens in their native language and responds to their queries in no time.
