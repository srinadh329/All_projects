Predictions

Multi-intent support
Most frameworks only support a single entity for each intent and hence, cannot easily handle natural requests with two intents. Our platform can recognise multiple intents from a single input and executes one task after the other, by transforming a complex utterance into two different sentences, each of them containing a single intent.
Scalable multi-level user intent hierarchy
We implemented a unique multi-level hierarchy to handle thousands of end-user intents with a remarkably low amount of training data required. It is highly scalable and very robust, giving support to billions of messages and users, growing with your business demand.
Conversational context understanding
Our chatbot has specific natural language processing (NLP) capabilities to understand the context of a conversation in multiple languages. It can also identify the intent of a question to provide an accurate first response and also propose options to confirm or clarify intent. It has advanced conversational capabilities and can proactively seek out information and can also ask clarifying questions even if the conversation is not linear.
Entity extraction for APIs
Identifying and extracting entities (people, places, companies, etc..) is a necessary step to simplify downstream processing. We implemented several different methods like regex extraction, dictionary extraction, complex pattern-based extraction and statistical extraction.
Sentiment analysis of end users
The chatbot can infer customer personality traits and understand sentiment and tone during an interaction to deliver a personalised experience or escalate to a live-agent when necessary.
Compound word splitting
Compound words will need to be split into smaller parts to allow accurate NLP. We present an approach for knowledge free and unsupervised recognition of compound nouns for languages that use one-word compounds such as Germanic and Scandinavian languages. Our approach works by creating a candidate list of compound splits based on the word list of a large corpus.
