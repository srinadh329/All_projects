Polling

Polls are a great way of giving your customers a voice and the feeling that their opinion truly counts towards your products or services.

They improve your marketing strategy and boost your brand on several platforms to drive product decisions and increase engagements.

Usage of polls will be met with improved response rates as people love to take part in votes that make a real difference.

Polling comes in handy since it encourages engagement and improves the visibility of your brand. Polls can also be used to help brands make more immediate decisions, with users aware that they have the power to create a specific outcome.

Data collection

By posing a quick question, your brand will be able to get instant feedback from customers about the products and services you offer. If users feel that their opinions are valued, then they are more likely to be loyal to your brand.

User authentication

When a customer has been identified and authenticated, the virtual assistant has the necessary permission to efficiently assist them with requests that would normally require them to use online banking.

Decision making

You can determine which products or promos interest your target audience right away. This real-time information can be crucial for driving product decisions and possible sales lead for your company.

Increase engagement

We make sure to be responsive to every comment or like, even after the poll has ended, to keep up a dialogue between you and your audience and increase the likelihood of future engagement.
