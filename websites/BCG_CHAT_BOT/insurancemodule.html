Virtual Insurance

Our agent driven by AI and integrated with social media platforms or retail websites can help insurers facilitate discussions with customers to determine their needs.

Using APIs integrated with ever-growing insurance technologies and third-party services for both data and the capabilities, make customers aware of how their needs can be met, personalize the products and simplify the application process.

Our huge intents guide customers through a simplified process to get life insurance coverage.

Two of the most important factors defining the efficiency of an insurance business is how fast it manages to settle claims, and how successfully it does it. Introduction of AI dramatically boosts both of these factors.

Claims-specific intents

Chatbot responds quickly to every type of customer query. Our huge intents let customers enter the details of belongings that have been stolen or lost for getting their claim approved in no time.

Streamline tedious processes

Capability to cut down on redundant paperwork and unnecessary steps in by putting complex terms into more straightforward language and walking new customers through simple steps.

User authentication

Let industries utilize our virtual agent to handle customer queries, recommending products and services best suited to customer needs, anticipating future requests and anticipating factors that might cause customer churn.

Always being present

Immediacy is a critical component of client satisfaction and when the human can have the right information at exactly the right time, insurers and claimants receive better service.
